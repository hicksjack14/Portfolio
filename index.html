001 — ABOUT

I am a junior at Syracuse University studying Television, Radio and Film at Newhouse. I grew up in a small town in Connecticut with cerebral palsy, played a lot of soccer, and spent most of my time singing and beatboxing. Senior year I led my school's a cappella group, got deeper into music, and started getting genuinely curious about how the industry works. That curiosity is what brought me to Syracuse and eventually to talent representation.

Right now I am co-founding M&H Inc. with Nehru Madan, a student agency where we rep Isabella Allon. I am Technical Director and talent booker on Loud and Clear, a live production on Orange Television Network. And I am on the socials board at University Union, which a lot of the time just means going out on campus and talking to people. I am still figuring a lot of it out, but I am doing the work.

My love of sports and storytelling pulled me into production. At the core of all of it is curiosity. I think perspective and empathy are the most valuable things you can collect, and that music, TV, film, and social media are the best ways to get them.

A Note on Process

This portfolio was built end-to-end using Claude Code, Anthropic's AI-powered CLI. Every line of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript was written, debugged, and iterated through natural language prompting. The Three.js animation, GSAP panel system, responsive layout, and design system all came out of a real collaborative workflow.

That's the skill on display here. Not just the output, but knowing what to ask for, reading the code, and steering the process toward something that actually feels like you. AI prompting done with intention is a legitimate creative and technical capability, and this site is the proof.
